Guide: Prof. Sudheesh Chethil, Indian Institute of Space Science and Technology, India

Studied the dynamics of entangled states generated in a beam splitter with a Kerr medium.

Entanglement dynamics of initial classical and non classical states are studied and compared.

Signatures of revival and fractional revival phenomena exhibited during the time evolution of states in the Kerr medium are captured in the entangled states produced by the beam splitter.

Observed that maximum entanglement is obtained at the instants of collapses of wave packets in the medium

Obtained results have been submitted to Modern Physics Letters B
